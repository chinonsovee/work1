    MARCH: BRAZILIAN



    Thursday, Mar 7, 6PM

    Vibe on the vibrant spirit of Brazil in March with an
    electrifying Supper Club odyssey.
    Smoked Chicken Coxhina
    Start your Brazilian culinary journey with the
    deli ghtful Coxhina – a smoked chicken croquette with
    a crispy exterior and a flavorful, savory filling
    Smoked Shrimp and Coconut Stew
    Delve into a rich and aromatic stew featuring smoky

    Wrap up your Brazilian feast with the sweet

    APRIL: MOROCCAN


    Thursday, Apr 11, 6PM

    Embark on an exotic escapade promising a feast that
    transports you to the heart of Morocco.
    Grilled Lamb Kefta
    Spiced ground lamb grilled to perfection and served
    with a refreshing herb yogurt dipping sauce, all
    accompanied by soft and warm flatbread

    Savor the vibrant flavors of Morocco with a carrot

    Engage in the exotic spices of Morocco with grilled

    A dessert that combines the warmth of Moroccan
    spices with the citrusy brightness of oranges,
